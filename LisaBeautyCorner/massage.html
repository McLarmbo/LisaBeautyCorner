We offer the following massages.

Prenatal Massage

Tailored for expectant mothers, prenatal massage helps alleviate discomfort associated with pregnancy, such as back pain and swelling. It is performed with special care to ensure the safety of both mother and baby.

Hot Stone Massage

In this technique, smooth, heated stones are placed on specific points of the body to warm up tight muscles and promote relaxation. The therapist may also use the stones to massage the body.

Deep Tissue Massage

This massage focuses on the deeper layers of muscle and connective tissue. It uses slower strokes and more intense pressure to relieve chronic pain and tension, making it ideal for athletes or those with tight muscles.


Aromatherapy

This type combines essential oils with massage techniques to enhance relaxation and emotional well-being. The oils are chosen based on their therapeutic properties and can help alleviate stress and anxiety.
