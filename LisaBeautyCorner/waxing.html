Types of waxing we offer.

Strip waxing

Strip waxing, also known as soft waxing, is one of the most common methods. Here’s how it works:

  • A thin layer of wax is applied to the skin.

  • Someone presses cloth strip or paper on top and quickly removes it, pulling the hair with it.

Hot Waxing

Hot waxing is another popular option that is gentle on the skin.

  • Heat the wax to a thick consistency and apply it directly to the skin.

  • It will harden as it cools. Then pull it out without using a bandage.

Sugar Waxing

Sugar waxing is a natural alternative made from sugar lemon and hot water.

  • Apply powdered sugar to the skin and remove it in the direction of hair growth.

Chocolate Waxing

Chocolate waxing is a luxurious alternative to hair removal.

  • Made from chocolate, cocoa, and almond oil. Which helps relax and moisturize.
